    Tips for choosing the best betting site

    • Know what you want in an online betting site. Think about your betting profile before anything else. What sports and leagues do you bet on? Do you need lots of options for props and derivatives? Do you usually bet on mobile or desktop? Knowing the answers to these types of questions will help you narrow down your sportsbook list.

    • Do your research. Make sure you read a few of our betting site reviews so you know what’s out there before you make your choice. Be sure to check our blacklist as well if you’re considering a sportsbook that isn’t on our recommended list.

    • Read the bonus terms and conditions. Big bonuses are often best, but we have seen bonuses that are too good to be true. Be sure to check the rollover and odds requirements before taking any sportsbook bonus.

    • Check the payout options and speed. Being able to get your winnings out promptly should be one of the first things you look at when choosing am online sports betting platform.
